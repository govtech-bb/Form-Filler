# Form Filler — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 3 June 2026_

Form Filler is a browser extension built by **GovTech Barbados** to help staff
test online forms by filling fields with randomly generated sample data.

## What data we collect

**None.** Form Filler does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any
personal information or browsing data.

- All test data (names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, dates, etc.) is
  generated **locally in your browser** at the moment you trigger the extension.
- The extension reads form fields on the current page **only** so it can fill
  them, and **only** when you actively trigger it (toolbar click or the
  Ctrl+Shift+F / Ctrl+Shift+X keyboard shortcuts).
- The only thing saved is a small local preference (the invalid-data test-mode
  toggle and the result of your last fill), stored on your own device via the
  browser's `chrome.storage.local`. It never leaves your device.
- No data is sent to GovTech Barbados or any third party. The extension makes
  no network requests.

## Permissions

Form Filler requests the minimum permissions needed to fill forms on the page
you are testing: `activeTab`, `scripting`, `tabs`, `storage`, and host access
to web pages (so it can operate on whichever site you choose to test).
